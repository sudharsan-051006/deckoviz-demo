Every meaningful thing we’ve built in our lives started with a feeling that wouldn’t go away.

A quiet discomfort.  
A sense that something important was missing.

Deckoviz began like that.

Not as a product idea.  
Not as a business plan.

But as a question we kept returning to:

Why do our homes — the places where our lives actually happen — feel so untouched by beauty, intention, and emotional intelligence?

We had more screens than ever.  
More apps. More automation.

Yet our spaces still felt… inert.
